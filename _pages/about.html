Our Story

Our fermented chilli products are inspired by an old family recipe, which has been enjoyed for many generations, and now we wish to share it with the world. We manufacture all of our products in small batches and the highest quality ingredients in the hinterlands of Byron Bay. Our goal is to bring a great chilli product to the world and to add a bit more flavour and excitment into your favourite meals.
