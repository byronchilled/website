Fermented Chilli Dip - Hot

Our gourmet Fermented Chilli Dip - Hot product is hand made in our kitchen in the hinterlands of Byron Bay using a traditional home-style recipe. We carefully select our high-quality, locally sourced chillis to undergo our signature fermentation process before blending them with a selection of fresh and organic ingredients for your enjoyment.

This gourmet product is best served on top of your favourite home-cooked and take-away meals, but can also be used in cooking for a hot spicy flavour.

Ingredients: fresh chilli (93%), organic coriander (4%), salt (2%), garlic (< 1%)
